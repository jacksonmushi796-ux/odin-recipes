Today we bring to you the "Chicken stir", a most common recipe and desert made of composite ingredients such as cheese, ginger and Irish potatoes all in one to create the most delicious desert in the world

The following are some of our key ingredients of our recipe

  • Chicken Steak
  • Chicken Masala
  • Cooking Oil
  • Chicken Sauce

Steps For Preparing a Chicken stir

  1. Prepare the meat sauce
  2. Make the white sauce
  3. Cook the Pasta Sheets
  4. Layer properly
    • Start with a thin layer of sauce
    • Add pasta sheets
    • Add meat sauce
    • Add white sauce
    • Sprinkle cheese

      • Repeat layers. Finish with cheese on top. Structure matters, random layering gives you a messy result

  5. Bake and rest

    6. Bake at ~180 degrees celcius for about 30 to 40 minutes until golden. Then let it rest for 10 to 15 minutes before cutting. Skipping this and it collapses into a sloppy mess.

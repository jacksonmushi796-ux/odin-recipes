Steps For Preparing a Spagheti

  1. Prepare the meat sauce
  2. Make the white sauce
  3. Cook the Pasta Sheets
  4. Layer properly
    • Start with a thin layer of sauce
    • Add pasta sheets
    • Add meat sauce
    • Add white sauce
    • Sprinkle cheese

      • Repeat layers. Finish with cheese on top. Structure matters, random layering gives you a messy result

  5. Bake and rest

    6. Bake at ~180 degrees celcius for about 30 to 40 minutes until golden. Then let it rest for 10 to 15 minutes before cutting. Skipping this and it collapses into a sloppy mess.
